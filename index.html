About Global Health & Wellness Social Welfare Foundation

GHWSWF is a registered Nigerian NGO (CAC IT-184957) based in Abuja. We drive healthcare access, food relief, and empowerment for the less privileged across Nigeria, guided by values of dignity, collaboration, and transparent stewardship.

Registered on July 26, 2022, GHWSWF operates with a board of Trustees and a growing network of volunteers who bring free medical outreach, nutrition support, and digital inclusion programs to communities in need.

CAC: IT-184957 Headquarters: Abuja, Nigeria

Our Programs

Five core initiatives delivering healthcare, nutrition, empowerment, and digital inclusion.

Primary Healthcare Outreaches

Free clinics, fever screening, immunization drives, and mobile care units in underserved communities.

Impact: 12,000+ lives touched

Maternal & Child Health Support

Antenatal care, nutrition, and newborn care in hospitals and outreach posts.

Impact: 5,000+ mothers cared for

Food & Welfare Relief

Relief packages, nutrition kits, and community feeding programs.

Impact: 27 communities served

Digital Health & ICT Inclusion

Telemedicine access, digital literacy, and e-health record awareness.

Impact: 2,500+ digital exchanges

Women & Girls Empowerment

Skills training, mentorship, and leadership development for girls and women.

Impact: 1,000+ empowered
